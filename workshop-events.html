Continuing education is an important aspect of working in any industry, but especially in tech because tools and resources are contantly evolving. Below is a list of resources for staying connected and informed of the everchanging updates in the tech industry.

Seattle Workshop Events

  • Better Engineering Better Engineering courses are taught by instructors who are in the industry on topics ranging from the future of computing to tech interviews.
  • Linux Fest Northwest (April) Every April Bellingham Tech hosts LinuxFest Northwest. Although this is outside Seattle, it's a very fun and interesting conference.
  • Ada's Technical Bookstore Events Calendar Ada's is a technical bookstore with workshops and networking events. Some of the events cost money.
  • Living Computer Museum + Labs The Living Computer Museum + Labs offers fun workshops on topics ranging from tech history to Raspberry Pi.
  • UW Events UW offers lectures and workshops that are sometimes open to the public. Occasionally the events require an admission price.
  • Seattle Tech Workshops Tech workshops recommended by Kathryn Brookshier (many of these workshops require a fee).
