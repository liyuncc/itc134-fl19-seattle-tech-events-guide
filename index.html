Tech Networking Events in Seattle

Whether you're new to Seattle, attending a coding bootcamp, or are in school, building a network of connections in the tech community can bolster a successful career in tech. Seattle has a trove of resources and companies. Meeting the right people can help you find your dream job. Take advantage of living in Seattle and check out some of the networking events around the city!

Seattle Networking Events

  • GeekWire GeekWire is a great place to find free tech community events with an array of different topics.
  • Ada's Technical Bookstore Events Calendar Ada's is a technical bookstore with workshops and networking events. Some of the events cost money.
  • New Tech Seattle Meetup Events New Tech Seattle's networking events usually cost about $10. The events are usually hosted by local companies and local startup founders frequently speak at the event.
  • Seattle Tech Meetup Groups Whether your interest is Python or AI ethics, you'll meet like-minded people in Seattle's tech Meetup.com community.
  • Fred Hutch Events & Lectures If you're interested in biological sciences and overlapping STEM fields, Fred Hutch offers fascinating lectures.
  • Built in Seattle Events Built in Seattle offers all sorts of events, whether you're interested in a hackathon or a workshop on how to improve your startup pitchdeck.
